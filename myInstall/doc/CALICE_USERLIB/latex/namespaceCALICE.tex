Calculate the drift velocity from the measured drift time of two adjacent chambers (same axis). Assuming that the particle track is reasonably parallel to the z-\/axis, the electrons generated in two adjacent chambers (containing wires pointing in the same direction) will travel in total the distance (in the drift direction) between the adjacent wires.
